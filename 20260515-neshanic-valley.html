Important Notes

  • Layout Selection: Neshanic Valley consists of three 9-hole courses: The Ridge, The Lake, and The Quarry. The pairing of courses will be announced by the pro shop upon arrival.
  • Check-in: Please arrive by 1:10 PM to allow ample time for check-in and warm-up.
  • Dining: We'll head to dinner immediately after the round. Options include the onsite Grill at Neshanic Valley or the nearby Riverside Inn in Neshanic Station.
  • Practice Facilities: Neshanic features a world-class Learning Center with a double-ended driving range and large putting greens.
  • Attire: Standard golf attire (collared shirts, no denim) is required.

Important Notes

  • Theme: Homage to Scotland (or Celtic Celebration) - feel free to highlight Ballyowen's Scottish/Irish links-inspired layout! Bagpipes NOT required...
  • Driving Directions: Please set your GPS to **Ballyowen Golf Club** (105-137 Wheatsworth Road, Hamburg, NJ), which is part of the Crystal Springs Resort.
  • Arrival Time: Everyone should plan to arrive at Ballyowen Golf Club by 11:30 AM to allow time for check-in, warm-up, and organization before the tee time.
  • Tee Times: Our tee times are 12:40 PM and 12:50 PM. Please be ready at the tee box at least 10 minutes prior.
  • Green Fee: The green fee is $109/pp and includes a cart. There is no separate walking rate for this round. (SOURCE: Ballyowen Golf Club)
  • Driving Range: Ballyowen Golf Club features practice facilities including a driving range, putting greens, and chipping area. (SOURCE: Ballyowen Golf Club)
  • Course Layout: The course layout returns to the clubhouse after 9 holes for an opportunity to use restrooms and access amenities like Owen's Pub. Ballyowen Golf Club features a links-style layout with open meadows and rolling farmland, offering 360-degree views. (SOURCE: Ballyowen Golf Club)
  • Amenities: The course features a Pro Shop, Lockers, Showers, and Owen's Pub (restaurant, bar, snack bar). Beverage carts are also available. (SOURCE: Ballyowen Golf Club)

Important Notes

  • Driving Directions: Please set your GPS to Black Bear Golf Course (114 Route 23 North, Franklin, NJ 07416).
  • Arrival Time: Plan to arrive at Black Bear Golf Course by 11:00 AM to allow time for check-in and warm-up.
  • Tee Time: Our tee time is 12:30 PM. Please be ready at the tee box at least 10 minutes prior.
  • Green Fee: The green fee is $90.50 per person and includes a cart (This was a GolfNow Hot Deal tee time, so the price is lower than typical published rates). There is no separate walking rate for this round. (SOURCE: Black Bear Golf Course - Rates)
  • Driving Range: Black Bear Golf Course features a lighted double-ended driving range on-site, as well as putting and chipping greens. (SOURCE: Black Bear Golf Course)
  • Course Layout: The course layout returns to the clubhouse after 9 holes for an opportunity to use restrooms and re-stock. Black Bear offers an inviting diversity of terrain, scenery, and shot selections across its 18 holes. (SOURCE: Black Bear Golf Course)
  • Amenities: The course features the Bear Den Grille (restaurant and bar), outdoor pool tables, and dart boards. (SOURCE: Black Bear Golf Course)

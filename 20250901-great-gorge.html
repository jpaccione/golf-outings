Important Notes

  • Driving Directions: Please set your GPS to Great Gorge Golf Club (414 County Route 517, Hamburg, NJ).
  • Arrival Time: Everyone should plan to arrive at Great Gorge Golf Club by 9:00 AM to allow time for check-in, warm-up, and organization before the tee time.
  • Tee Times: Our tee times are 10:00 AM, 10:10 AM, 10:20 AM, and 10:30 AM. Please be ready at the tee box at least 10 minutes prior to your assigned time.
  • Green Fee: The green fee is **$100/pp and includes a cart**. (SOURCE: Great Gorge Golf Club - Passes & Packages)
  • Driving Range: There is an expansive practice area on-site featuring a full driving range, putting green, and chipping area. (SOURCE: Great Gorge Golf Club - Driving Range)
  • Course Layout: Great Gorge Golf Club features challenging terrain with dramatic elevation changes and breathtaking mountain views, across its three 9-hole courses (Rail, Lake, and Quarry). (SOURCE: Great Gorge Golf Club - Our Courses)
  • Amenities: The modern clubhouse features a well-stocked pro shop and Hef's Hut Bar & Grill, a full-service restaurant. (SOURCE: Hef's Hut Bar and Grill)

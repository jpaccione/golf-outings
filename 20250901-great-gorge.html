Important Notes

  • Driving Directions: Please set your GPS to **Great Gorge Golf Club** (414 County Route 517, Hamburg, NJ).
  • Arrival Time: Plan to arrive at Great Gorge Golf Club by 09:00 AM to allow time for check-in and warm-up.
  • Tee Times: Our tee times are 10:00 AM, 10:10 AM, 10:20 AM, and 10:30 AM. Please be ready at the tee box at least 10 minutes prior to your assigned tee time.
  • Course Assignment: Great Gorge has 27 holes. We will be assigned to one of the 18-hole layouts upon arrival.
  • Green Fee: The average green fee is $100 per person, which includes a cart.
  • Driving Range: Great Gorge Golf Club offers a practice facility.
  • Course Layout: Please inquire at the pro shop upon arrival for details about the specific 18-hole course layout assigned for our round.
  • Amenities: The club features a pro shop and a restaurant/bar for food and beverages.
